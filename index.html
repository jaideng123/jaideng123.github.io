Speedbutton

  • An Android game where you tap buttons as quickly as possible in various game modes. created over the course of 24 hours for TamuHack 2014 (a Hackathon hosted at Texas A&M).

RSA and Stegonography

  • An RSA and Stegonography tool created in C++ for encrypting and decrypting plaintext created in conjunction with 5 other team mates as a final project for CSCE 315 (Programming Studio).

The software itself uses CPPunit to accommodate test driven development and GMP for performing operations on big integers. I organized Scrums, maintained the backlog, and designed and implemented the RSA system.
